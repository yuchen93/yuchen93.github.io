  • CS269: AI Applications in Robotics (fall 2024)
    This course provides an in-depth exploration of modern artificial intelligence (AI) techniques and their applications in robotics by surveying recent research. Students will gain comprehensive knowledge of the integration of AI algorithms and robotic systems to create intelligent, autonomous machines. This course covers topics across computer vision (robot perception), natural language processing (NLP), robotic control, imitation learning, reinforcement learning (RL), and human-robot interaction (HRI). By the end of the course, students will have the skills to design, implement, and evaluate AI solutions in various robotic applications.
